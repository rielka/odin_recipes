Greek Salad

Description

This is a typical good Greek salad. It consists of fresh vegetables, olives and feta cheese.

This Greek salad is easy to prepare and smells incredibly good. It is worth giving it a try, especially in the summer time!

Ingredients

  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 (6 ounce) can pitted black olives
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a large salad bowl put all the prepared ingredients together.
  2. Mix the olive oil, oregano, lemon juice and black pepper together and dress the salad with the obtained mixture.
