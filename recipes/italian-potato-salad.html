Italian Potato Salad

Description

This potato salad is easy to prepare and smells excellent. With the time it becomes my favourite one.

I offer or serve it whenever I get visit with the so called "Wiener Wurst" (Wiener sausage).

Ingredients

  • 5 large potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ⅔ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ cup white wine vinegar
  • ⅓ cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoesand cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain, cool and chop.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the garlic, olive oil, vinegar and parsley. Add potatoes and toss to evenly coat.
  3. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
