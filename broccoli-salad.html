Broccoli Salad

Description

This ist a yummy summer broccoli salad. It consists of a combination of fruits, vegetables and meats. The dressing is always made of olive oil, salt, pepper and lemon juice but you can vary it however you like.

Brocolli salad is easy to prepare and smells very interesting. It is worth giving it a try!

Ingredients

  • 200 grams chicken meat (or bacon)
  • 2 heads fresh broccoli
  • 1 small red onion
  • ¾ cup raisins
  • ¾ cup sliced almonds
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon of olive oil and 1 of lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Cut the chicken meat (or bacon) into small pieces and roast them in a skillet under medium temperature until they becom done.
  2. Cut the broccoli and the onion into bite-sized pieces. Mix them with the Chicken (or bacon) pieces, the raisins and the almonds.
